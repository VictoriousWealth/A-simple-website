  • GDPR, Terms&Conditions, Privacy Policy and Data Storage Policy

    • Adhering to the General Data Protection Regulation released in May 2018, I have to declare certain statements.
    I am Nick Efe Oni, a student at the University Of Sheffield, and any data collected from you is collectd via a form, is minimal and proportioned to the task at hand. Also, it won't be shared with people that were not intended to be shared with unless stated specifically otherwise.
    Now, if I were to turn this into a business, I would have to:

    • register with the ICO (Information Commissioners Office);
    • estabilish a DPO (Data Protection Officer);
    • specify exactly what information I collect, why, and who do I share it with;
    • Pay an annual fee;
    • Report to ICO any data breach.
